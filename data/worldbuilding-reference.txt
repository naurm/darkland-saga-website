== SERIES ==

### Darkness Kindled (Book 1 on Royal Road - free)
Synopsis: For years, Fior has longed to strike back against his father's killer. But beyond the Pillar of Light's protective shroud, survival itself is a fool's hope. When the Crown Prince offers Fior a chance to strike behind enemy lines, he seizes it without hesitation. But before the mission can begin, darkness-cloaked agents unleash a series of attacks within the Capitol itself. When one attacks Fior's closest friend, he is drawn into a sinister conspiracy. Determined not to lose his friend, Fior will foil the Storm's plot at any cost. But the deeper he descends into the dark conspiracy, the clearer one terrible truth becomes: saving his friend may mean sacrificing an entire kingdom to death incarnate.

### Hunting Misfortune (Prequel, set 5 years before Darkness Kindled)
Synopsis: Beyond Light's reach, monsters known as bane claim the shadows. Scarred by failure, Rowan, a seasoned banehunter, wants nothing more than to work alone. But when a hunt in Eventide Mine collides with a hellcat and a mysterious rival hunter known only as "Misfortune," betrayal shatters Rowan all over again—and reveals a threat far darker than the monsters he hunts. Battered and broken, Rowan follows the fading trail of Misfortune into the path of the Umbra Six, an elite squad sworn to the dark cult known as the Blackstones. As old wounds reopen and loyalties are tested, Rowan must decide whether Misfortune is beyond saving—or whether redemption is still possible.

== WORLD: EADROM ==

The world of Eadrom is covered in perpetual darkness by The Storm. The last refuge is Arsatir, protected by the mystical Pillar of Light. Four nations:

- **Ciallmhar** (North East) - Fire affinity, capital. Large inland sea, Woods of Wisdom, The Smoldering Forest (home to Os, The Firehart).
- **Oroa** (North West) - Earth affinity. Mountainous, Fallen Mountains (some float due to earthstone concentration).
- **Proteya** (West) - Water affinity. Coastal, naval power, maelstrom with dry center for waterstone mining.
- **The Wildwood** (South East) - Wind affinity. Forest, Griffon Riders, forest drakes.

== MAGIC SYSTEM ==

Essence stones hold raw elemental magic. Must be bonded (contact over time or forced bond). Weaving: golden thread of light connects bonded user to stone, commands form spells.

- Earth (white): Making whole, healing, protecting, building
- Wind (green): Creativity, openness, working with flow
- Water (blue): Trust, exploration, discovery, bravery
- Fire (red): Precision, discipline, control - creates fire, not just manipulates it

Darkstones (black/UV): Corrupted essence stones used by the Blackstones.
